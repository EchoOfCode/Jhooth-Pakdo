🛡️ About Jhooth Pakdo

Jhooth Pakdo ("Catch the Lie") is India's first citizen-first, AI-powered election misinformation firewall. In a landscape where ₹400+ crore was spent on AI-generated political content — much of it disinformation — there existed zero neutral tool for ordinary citizens to fight back.
